# Brand Architecture — Onda / Zuly

*Working brand system for the AI wellness companion. Prepared July 2026. Availability notes are directional (from public search), not a legal clearance opinion — confirm with a trademark attorney before adopting.*

---

## The system at a glance

| Element | Name | Role |
|---|---|---|
| **Platform** | **Onda** | The place / the vibe / the continuous wave (∞) |
| **Guide (flagship)** | **Zuly** | Your personal AI guide through the pillars and courses — Lupe's authentic voice |
| **Guide (Phase 2, men)** | **Cruz** | The male voice; same house, second persona |
| **Mark** | **∞ (lemniscate)** | The endless wave — check in, act, reflect, go again |
| **Course/program unit** | **a Wave** (or **a Set**) | One dynamically-generated program Zuly builds for you |
| **Domains** | **Pillars** | Mind, Body, Money, Purpose, etc. |

**One-line:** *Onda is where you are. Zuly is who's with you.*

---

## Why the concept is strong

- **The name is a warm check-in.** "¿Qué onda?" is the everyday Latino way of saying *what's up / how are you*; *buena onda* means good vibes. The brand literally greets you the way the product does.
- **It earns the ∞ mark.** A wave oscillates and returns endlessly — so the infinity logo describes the actual mechanic (assess → guide → act → reflect → repeat), not just decoration. A lemniscate is two joined loops, which also sets up the two-guide story (Zuly + Cruz).
- **It's authentically Lupe.** Latin heritage + her DJ/music world (waves, sound, "sets") give the identity a true origin rather than a focus-grouped one.
- **Clean role split.** A neutral platform + a named human guide is a proven architecture (Duolingo/Duo, Headspace/its guide). It scales, adds voices, and survives any single persona.

---

## Product vocabulary (unlocked by the wave idea)

- **A Wave** — one dynamically-built course/program. "Zuly's building you a Sleep Wave," "you finished your Money Wave." Clearer than "modules."
- **A Set** *(alternative)* — a deep-cut nod to Lupe's DJ life; Zuly "mixes you a set." Warmer and more ownable, slightly less literal.
- **Pillars** — the life domains Waves are built in (Mind, Body, Money, Purpose…).
- **Ride / momentum / flow** — natural motivational language the wave gives you for free.

## Availability check — the honest picture

**"Onda" is crowded in wellness/lifestyle.** This is the concern to resolve before committing.

- **ONDA Life: Flow & Focus** — an existing *mindfulness* app (HRV breathing, "flow & focus"), ONDA LIFE, INC. Direct category collision, same "flow" language. [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/onda-life/id6755912529) · [onda-life.com](https://onda-life.com/)
- **Onda Wellness** — plant-based/CBD wellness products company. [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/ondawellness)
- **Onda Beauty** (clean-beauty retailer), **Onda** tequila seltzer, **ONDA** members club app (lifestyle). Multiple live marks in adjacent classes.
- **Domains:** `onda.app` listed for sale (aftermarket); `onda.com` not cleanly available. Exact-match is gone or premium-priced. [onda.app](https://onda.app/)

**"Zuly" is comparatively clean.** Closest hit is **Zulily** (different spelling, e-commerce/retail class) — likely not a blocker in health software. Distinctive, ownable, authentically hers.

## Recommendations

### ▶ Recommended: Path A — Zuly as the master brand, "onda" as the identity
Make **Zuly** the company/app trademark (it cleared well). Keep **onda**, the wave, *buena onda*, and the ∞ mark as the verbal + visual identity and voice — not the legal name. You keep every bit of the warmth and the "¿qué onda?" hook, lose the trademark headache, and own your name.
- App/company: **Zuly**
- Female guide: **Zuly** · Male guide (Phase 2): **Cruz**
- Voice/identity: *buena onda*, waves, ∞
- **Best for:** shipping fast with a defensible, ownable brand.

### Path B — Keep "Onda" but differentiate and clear it
Stay with Onda, accept a modifier or coined variant plus a non-exact domain (`getonda`, `onda`+word, `.co`), and budget for trademark coexistence/clearance given ONDA Life and Onda Wellness.
- **Best for:** if the team is emotionally set on Onda and willing to spend on legal + a compromised domain.
- **Risk:** launching beside an existing mindfulness app of nearly the same name; brand never fully ownable.

### Path C — Swap the wave-name for a cleaner one: **Marea**
**Marea** = Spanish/Italian for *tide* — the wave that endlessly returns (perfect ∞ fit), feminine, warm, on-heritage, and far less crowded than Onda. "The tide" carries the reinvention arc beautifully (you rise, fall, rise again). Same system: **Marea** (platform) + **Zuly** (guide) + **Cruz** (Phase 2).
- Other wave/vibe candidates if Marea is taken: **Corriente** (current), **Vibra** (vibe), **Ola** (wave), **Ondina** (little wave).
- **Best for:** wanting the exact Onda feeling with a clear runway. *(Needs its own availability pass.)*

4. Draft the onboarding + "¿qué onda?" first-session flow in Zuly's voice.
